Calculator

The 'eval' applet will evaluate simple mathematical expressions. For example:

    3*4+5^2/7
    sin 2 pi
    sqrt 1.3

monadic operators have the same functions as those in J:

    +48.7 = 48.7
    - -2 = 2
    /3 = 1/3
    *-4 = -1 (signum)
    ^ 3 = e^3

Equation Grapher

The equation grapher uses the same syntax as the calculator to plot equations in cartesian or polar coordinates. Which one you get is determined by the "y=" / "r=" choice on the left.

The grapher expects equations solved in terms of y or r, as a function of x or theta respectively. Note that you may plot several equations at once, and that you may move the position of the graph by dragging it.

The scale adjustement (at the bottom) is a magnification factor, with '1' being 1 pixel per unit. The larger the magnification, the bigger the picture.

sample equations:

    y= -x^3 + 3x^2 + 7x - 7
    y= x + 5 sin x
    y=sqrt 256 - x^2
    y=(sin x) + (sin 2x) / 2 + (sin 3x) / 3 + (sin 4x) / 4

    r= 2 + sin 5 theta r= sin 3 theta
    r= 7 sin theta/2
